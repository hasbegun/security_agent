# Phishing Response Playbook

## Objective
To provide clear steps for employees to identify and report suspected phishing emails.

## Steps for Employees
1.  **DO NOT** click any links.
2.  **DO NOT** reply to the email.
3.  **DO NOT** open any attachments.
4.  **DO** immediately forward the suspected email as an attachment to `security@eos.ai` or `security@mycompany.com`.
5.  After forwarding, **delete** the suspicious email from your inbox.
